## FORMS
+ 'fieldset ' - groups number of inputs together
+ 'autocomplete="on" '
+ 'autofocus ' - when the page loads
+ 'pattern="" ' - 
+ ** pattern = "REGEX"**:
	- [0-9] Number from 0-9
	- [A-Z] Uppercase Letter
	- [a-z] Lowercase Letter
	- [A-Za-z] Uppercase or Lowercase
	- {1} a single instance of
	- {3} sequence of three
+ type="range" min="0" max="100"

	<select id="currency">
